# Feature Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP, Design + Marketing Deliverable Factory

- Structured request intake for Design + Marketing
- Agent Owner/Cluster execution with production recipes
- Template library with consistent output schemas
- Human review, QA checklist, approvals
- Export to Google Doc/PDF
- Tenant isolation, RBAC, audit logs

## Phase 2: Company-Controlled Agent Behavior

- Admin console for recipes, templates, guardrails
- Per-client context vault with tighter scoping
- Better packaging (handoff summaries, rationale, variant selection)
- Basic analytics dashboard

## Phase 3: Expand Deliverables and Teams

- Add Strategy and Sales deliverables (briefs, scripts, proposals)
- Add Development support outputs (tickets, specs, QA notes)
- Integrations with CRM, storage, project tools

## Phase 4: Enterprise and Scale

- Advanced governance (policy packs, approvals by risk level)
- Client portal delivery and permissions
- Capacity and quality scoring
- Marketplace or library of approved recipes and templates
